Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I complete a survey?

To complete a survey:

  1. Go to "Available Surveys" from your dashboard
  2. Click "Take Survey" on any available survey
  3. Answer all questions completely
  4. Click "Submit Survey" to complete

Make sure to complete surveys before their deadline expires!

How do I earn tokens?

You earn tokens by completing surveys that offer rewards. Look for surveys with token badges in the "Available Surveys" section. Tokens are automatically added to your account upon survey completion.

What's the difference between daily and biweekly surveys?

Daily surveys are available for 24 hours from their start time, while biweekly surveys remain available for one full week. Make sure to complete them within their respective time limits.

Can I change my answers after submitting?

No, once you submit a survey, your responses cannot be changed. Please review your answers carefully before submitting.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to "Reset Password" from your dashboard. You'll need to enter your current password and choose a new one.
